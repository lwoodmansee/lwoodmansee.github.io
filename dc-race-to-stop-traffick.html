Washington D.C. Race to Stop Traffick

Sponsored by Klima Orthodontics

Come join us as we "Race to Stop Traffick" on Saturday, October 7 at Lake Accotink Park in Northern Virginia.





Frequently Asked Questions

What is Operation Underground Railroad?

Since 2013, Operation Underground Railroad (O.U.R.) has gathered the world's experts in extraction operations and anti-child trafficking efforts to bring an end to child slavery. O.U.R. rescues victims and works with local resources in a comprehensive process involving justice for the perpetrators and recovery and rehabilitation for the survivors. Learn more at ourrescue.org.

When is the race?

The event will be held on Saturday, October 7, 2017 at 9:00 a.m. at Lake Accotink Park in Northern Virginia.

What time should I be there on race day?

Runners should arrive between 8:00 a.m. and 8:30 a.m. for check in and packet pick up. The 1-Mile Fun Run will begin at 9:00 a.m. and the 5k race will begin at 9:30 a.m.

Where is the race?

Both races will take place at Lake Accotink Park (5660 Heming Ave., Springfield, VA) and will start at the McLaren shelter. The parking lot will be clearly marked and parking is free.

Are strollers allowed?

Strollers are welcome on the 1 mile but discouraged on the 5k.

What do I get for participating?

All participants will receive a t-shirt, tasty snacks to refuel and the opportunity to participate in family-friendly activities after the run. Prizes will be awarded to the top three male and top three female 5k runners. Top finishing children from the 5k and 1 Mile Fun Run will also be awarded prizes.

Are pets allowed?

Unfortunately we cannot accomodate pets at this event. Please do not bring them.

I’d like to come but I can’t. What should I do?

Abolitionists who want to participate in the liberation efforts but who aren't local can participate virtually! Virtual runners can run a 5K course on your own before or on the day of the race. Your race bib will be mailed to the address you provide when you register. Optional t-shirts will be mailed after the race has taken place. When you have completed your 5k Virtual Run, post a picture of you during/after the run with the hashtag #racetostoptraffick.

Can I volunteer at this event?

Yes! If you are interested in volunteering on race day, please contact us at racetostoptraffick [at] gmail [dot] com.
